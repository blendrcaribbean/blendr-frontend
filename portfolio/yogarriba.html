the challenge

To create a vibrant, energetic brand identity for a yoga studio in Bonaire that captured the spirit of wellness and the island lifestyle.

our approach

Yogarriba needed a brand that communicated vitality, wellness, and the beautiful surroundings of Bonaire. We developed a complete brand package including business cards, social media templates, and marketing materials. The visual language drew from the natural beauty of the island, combining tropical energy with the mindfulness of yoga practice.
