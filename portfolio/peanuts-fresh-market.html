the challenge

To bring to life an innovative organic market cafe with an original concept.

our approach

Not only was Peanuts one of our first clients, but they also trusted in our capabilities without reservation, giving us full creative control over the brand. Their logo is a redesign of their initial logo which included the peanut artwork. It was very important to keep that as it represented their beginning. All the collateral and print media is designed in base to the original concept which was to bring about organic, homemade, and affordable products and cuisine to their customers.
