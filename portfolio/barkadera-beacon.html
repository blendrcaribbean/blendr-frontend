the challenge

To create a compelling brand identity and website for a new local news platform that felt authentic to Bonaire.

our approach

We crafted a brand identity that felt rooted in the island's culture while maintaining journalistic credibility. The website was designed for ease of navigation and content consumption, with copywriting that set the editorial tone for the publication.
